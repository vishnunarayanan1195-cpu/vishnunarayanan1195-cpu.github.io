About Ethno Organics

Kerala's Forest Products, Sourced with
Science, Legality, and Trust

Ethno Organics is a Kerala-based export partnership built by professionals with strong knowledge of forestry science, forest produce sourcing, organic products, and export documentation.

Who We Are

Ethno Organics was established with a clear purpose: to connect international buyers with authentic, legally sourced, and properly documented forest products and organic spices from Kerala.

We focus on products such as Indian sandalwood oil, Kerala black pepper, green cardamom, cinnamon, nutmeg, mace, clove, forest honey, wild botanicals, Ayurvedic herbs, resins, and other non-timber forest products. Our approach is built on transparency, scientific evaluation, and legal procurement.

As a newly launched business, we believe our greatest strength is not exaggerated claims, but our commitment to traceability, documentation, buyer confidence, and long-term supply relationships.

Our Foundation

Professional Expertise Behind Every Shipment

Our team brings knowledge of forestry science, species identification, sustainable harvesting practices, non-timber forest produce regulations, quality assessment, and export compliance.

This background helps us evaluate products beyond appearance alone. We focus on source authenticity, botanical identity, moisture levels, purity parameters, contamination risks, laboratory testing, and documentation readiness.

Our Sourcing Philosophy

Ethno Organics sources products only through authorised and legally acceptable channels. For regulated forest products, we work with authorised government and licensed supply systems. For spices and organic products, we work with certified farmers, approved merchants, and reliable producer networks.

Our aim is to protect both the buyer and the ecosystem. We do not support illegal forest extraction, undocumented trading, or misleading product claims. Every genuine buyer deserves clarity on where the product comes from, how it is tested, and what documentation supports the shipment.

Testing & Documentation

Certification and Quality Assurance

Our certification and documentation system is designed to give buyers confidence before shipment.

🔬 Laboratory Testing

Product testing is conducted through recognised laboratories, including testing support from IWST Bangalore where applicable. Testing may include purity, moisture, microbial parameters, pesticide residue, heavy metals, essential oil content, and buyer-specific requirements.

📋 Certificate of Analysis

A Certificate of Analysis can be provided for eligible consignments, covering relevant quality parameters based on the product category and destination market requirements.

🌿 Organic Documentation

Organic certification documentation is arranged for eligible products sourced from certified organic supply channels, including NPOP or equivalent documentation where applicable.

🛃 Export Documents

We support export documentation such as invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, phytosanitary certificate, fumigation certificate where required, and destination-specific compliance documents.

🏛️ KFDC / Legal Source Documentation

For sandalwood oil and regulated forest produce, legal source documentation is maintained through authorised supply channels, including KFDC-related documentation where applicable.

📦 Lot Traceability

Each shipment is managed with lot-level traceability, allowing buyers to connect product batches with source records, testing references, and export documentation.

Our Commitment to Buyers

We understand that trust is especially important when working with a new supplier. For this reason, Ethno Organics encourages trial orders, sample evaluation, document verification, and transparent communication before long-term supply commitments are made.

Our goal is to build lasting relationships with serious buyers who value genuine origin, legal sourcing, scientific quality assurance, and consistent export service.
