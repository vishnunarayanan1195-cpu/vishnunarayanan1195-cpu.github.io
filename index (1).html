Transparent Supply Chain

From Forest Record to Your Doorstep

We operate a fully traceable, legally compliant supply chain — built from the forest outward, not from the market backward. Every step is documented.

1
Legal Source Procurement

All products are procured through the Kerala Forest Development Corporation (KFDC), licensed forest produce depots, and NTFP-licensed tribal cooperatives — ensuring complete legality under the Indian Forest Act and relevant state regulations.

2
Scientific Field Evaluation

Our forestry-qualified team personally evaluates each lot at the source — assessing species authenticity, moisture levels, contamination, and visual grade parameters using scientific methodology, not guesswork.

3
NABL-Accredited Laboratory Testing

Every accepted batch is submitted to NABL-accredited laboratories for independent testing covering pesticide residues, heavy metals, microbial count, essential oil content, moisture, and buyer-specified export parameters.

4
Professional Packaging & Documentation

Food-safe, export-standard packaging with lot-coded traceability. Full documentation pack prepared including COA, phytosanitary certificate, certificate of origin, and any market-specific compliance documents.

5
Committed Consistent Dispatch

Pre-agreed supply schedules are honoured. We manage seasonal planning, harvest coordination, and buffer procurement to ensure reliable, uninterrupted supply for our buyer partners — regardless of season.

Our Authenticated Sources

We are transparent about where every product comes from. These are our primary authorised supply channels — all operating within the full framework of Indian forest and trade law.

🏛️
Kerala Forest Development Corporation (KFDC) The state government's authorised body for Kerala forest produce — our primary and most trusted source. KFDC certification guarantees complete product legality, traceability, and sustainable harvesting compliance. Essential for sandalwood oil and regulated forest produce.
🌿
NTFP-Licensed Tribal Cooperatives Partnerships with Scheduled Tribe communities holding government-issued Non-Timber Forest Produce collection licences. Ensures direct community benefit, authentic wild-organic character, and social compliance for ethical market buyers.
🌾
NPOP-Certified Organic Farmers Network of Wayanad, Idukki, and Thrissur farmers holding National Programme for Organic Production (NPOP) or equivalent certification. Verified organic management systems for spice products.
🏢
Registered Forest Produce Merchants Procurement via government-licensed forest produce merchants and registered depots holding complete transit permits, produce documentation, and legal authorisations under the Indian Forest Act.

Sandalwood Oil — GC-MS Analysis on Every Batch

Genuine Indian sandalwood oil (Santalum album) is among the world's most adulterated essential oils. We subject every batch to Gas Chromatography–Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS) analysis through recognised laboratories including IWST Bangalore, confirming α-Santalol (minimum 41%), β-Santalol, and full terpene profile in line with ISO 3518 — before any shipment is confirmed. Buyers receive the full GC-MS report alongside their order.
